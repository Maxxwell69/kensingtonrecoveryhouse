For yourself or a loved one

Apply for a bed.

If you can come in person, do that — it’s the fastest path. If you can’t, fill out this form or call us.

  • Walk in — 2418 Kensington Ave, Mon–Fri 9am–4pm.
  • Call — 215-917-7474, Mon–Fri 9am–4pm.
  • Email — FirstStopKensington@gmail.com.
  • Form — fill out the intake to the right and we’ll call you back.

We don’t require ID, insurance, or proof of income to start. Bring what you have. We’ll sort the rest.

For caseworkers, hospitals, parole, courts

Refer someone to us.

If you work with people who need a bed, we want to hear from you. We work with caseworkers, parole officers, hospital discharge teams, and community partners across Philadelphia.

  1. Complete the referral questionnaire to the left.
  2. Tell us if you need a callback — provide a number.
  3. We’ll get back to you the same business day, usually within hours.

Urgent? Call 215-917-7474 first; the form is for non-urgent referrals.

Partner with us

Hire a graduate. Open a door.

We work with a network of clinical, employer, and re-entry organizations across Philadelphia. If your work overlaps ours, let’s talk.

  • Employers — we have residents ready for shifts in kitchens, construction, warehouse, retail, and more.
  • Clinical partners — counseling and therapy organizations looking to serve our residents.
  • Re-entry & legal — organizations serving people returning from incarceration.
  • Community groups, churches, mutual aid — if you’re working this neighborhood, we should know each other.
